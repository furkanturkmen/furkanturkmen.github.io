CSS Animations

Fadein transition to all slides.

<article id="webslides">
  <section>
    <div class="wrap fadeInUp">
      <h1>Slide</h1>
    </div>
  </section>
</article>

Just 5 basic animations: .fadeIn, .fadeInUp, .zoomIn, .slideInLeft, and .slideInRight.
